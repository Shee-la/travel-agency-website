ALL INFORMATION ABOUT TRAVELLING

Travelling is one of the most exciting ways to explore the world and gain experiences that last a lifetime. The feeling of being able to discover new places, cultures, and people is unparalleled. Traveling can be a great way to make new friends, build relationships, and grow as a person. By taking the time to explore a new city, country, or continent, we are able to learn about different customs and cultures and gain insight on how other people live.

It can be an opportunity to learn about yourself and understand what matters to you most. Whether it’s a weekend getaway or an extended vacation, travelling can help us to expand our horizons and broaden our perspectives. So, don’t be afraid to take a chance and travel to new and exciting places. You never know what amazing experiences and memories you could gain.

5 key things to put into consideration before making travelling plans

1. Budget:
Before making any travel plans, it’s important to consider your available budget and plan accordingly. Taking into account potential travel costs, accommodation, food, and activities can help you to make a realistic budget for your trip.

2. Duration of Stay:
How long do you plan to be away and what kind of activities do you plan to do? This will help you to determine the best places to visit, the length of time you’ll need to stay at each destination, and the type of accommodations you may need.

3. Transportation:
How do you plan to get from one place to another? Will you be taking a plane, train, or other form of transportation? Consider the cost and convenience of each option and decide which one works best for your trip.

4. Accommodations:
Where will you stay? Will you be staying in a hotel, motel, or hostel? Will you need to book a reservation in advance? Depending on your budget, you may need to consider different types of accommodations.

5. Safety:
This is one of the most important factors to consider when planning a trip. Research the area you’ll be visiting and familiarize yourself with local laws and customs. Check for any travel advisories and make sure to purchase travel insurance in case of an emergency.
Fun fact about travelling
Travelling for just one hour can reset your body clock and reduce jet lag.


The most visited places on earth are popular tourist destinations due to their unique attractions and amenities. These places offer something special that many people find appealing and want to experience.
They may also have a strong cultural and historical significance, making them attractive to visitors from around the world.
Additionally, many of these places are easily accessible, making them popular choices for travelers looking for a convenient and exciting vacation.

The most visited places on earth in history with the estimated number of visitors

1. Grand Canyon National Park, USA - 6 million visitors per year.

Grand Canyon National Park is famous for its spectacular views, stunning landscapes, and its incredible array of wildlife.

2. Taj Mahal, India - 7-8 million visitors per year.

The Taj Mahal is a world-famous monument in India; it is known for its intricate architecture, beautiful gardens, and its symbol of love.

3. Machu Picchu, Peru - 1.2 million visitors per year.

Machu Picchu is an archaeological site that was built by the Inca civilization in the 15th century. It is famous for its incredible views of the surrounding mountains, as well as its many terraces and temples.

4. Vatican City, Italy - 6.3 million visitors per year.

Vatican City is known for being the home of the Roman Catholic Church and the seat of the Pope. It is also famous for its many artworks, sculptures, and religious artifacts.

5. Great Wall of China, China - 10 million visitors per year.

The Great Wall of China is one of the most impressive man-made structures in the world. It is famous for its sheer size, its age, and its construction techniques.

6. Petra, Jordan - 1.4 million visitors per year.

Petra is an ancient archaeological site in Jordan. It is famous for its sandstone carvings and monuments, many of which date back to the Roman era.

7. Great Pyramid of Giza, Egypt - 4.7 million visitors per year.

The Great Pyramid of Giza is the oldest and largest of the three pyramids in Giza. It is famous for being the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, as well as its incredible size and age.

8. Acropolis, Greece - 3 million visitors per year.

The Acropolis is an ancient citadel located in Athens, Greece. It is famous for its ancient Greek temples and monuments, such as the Parthenon.

9. Angkor Wat, Cambodia - 2.5 million visitors per year.

Angkor Wat is a temple complex located in Cambodia. It is famous for its intricate designs, carvings, and statues, many of which are still standing today.

10. Chichen Itza, Mexico - 2 million visitors per year.

Chichen Itza is an archaeological site located in Mexico. It is famous for its Mayan ruins, including the iconic El Castillo pyramid.
